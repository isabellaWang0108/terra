Maintaining consistent expression of the VTS brand is extended through colour usage. We are committed to the focused use of color in order to communicate exactly what is important for our users. Color supports the purpose of the content. They indicate systems like the hierarchy of information, interactive states, and the differences between distinctive elements, which then helps our user navigate through our product and recognize the actions that need to be taken. When working in the platform clarity is key, use neutral colors paired with brand or utility colors to highlight focal points.

Our color system is designed to follow the accessibility guidelines that meet WCAG 2.1 compliant contrast ratios. This ensures sufficient color contrast between elements so that the platform experience can be more accessible for people who are color blind or have low vision. # Brand color

Brand color plays an important role in Marketing and product design, through brand awareness and brand reinforcement; however it should be used sparingly across the product platform. Incorporate Brand color as accent colors with the use of a neutral color pallette. The Brand color can be used to indicate location, state, and call-to-action.
